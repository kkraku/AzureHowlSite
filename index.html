What is AzureHowl?

AzureHowl is an animated story about wolves.

A legend tells a story of two dragons which were once saved by a pack of wolves. When the dragons left this place they couldn't leave without thanking the wolves that saved them. The white wolf was a mother-like figure to them, so they stood in a cave to transform into stone and leave a special power which became present in every wolf and made the land split turning it into an island. This power was still present the later generations of wolves, and will be for a long time.
